Cable Actuator

The scene simulate a force exerted by a cable to solve a position effector.

It uses the QPInverseProblemSolver, CableActuator and PositionEffector components, available in the SoftRobots plugin.




How to

Select the sphere (shift + click left) and move it around. The effector (green square) will follow the sphere (if possible) by acting on the cable.

Further descriptions of the component can be found at: https://project.inria.fr/softrobot/documentation/
