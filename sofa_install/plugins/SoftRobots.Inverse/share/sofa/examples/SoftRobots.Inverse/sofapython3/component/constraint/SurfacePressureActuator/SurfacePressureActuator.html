Surface Pressure Actuator on cavity

The scene simulate a pressure on a cavity in the bunny body to solve a position effector. In this exemple we set a max pressure value.

It uses the QPInverseProblemSolver, SurfacePressureActuator and PositionEffector components, available in the SoftRobots plugin.



How to

Select the sphere (shift + click left) and move it around. The effector (green square) will follow the sphere (if possible) by applying pressure in the body cavity.

Further descriptions of the component can be found at: https://project.inria.fr/softrobot/documentation
